About Sange SA Student Portal

Our Mission

At Sange SA, our mission is to remain dynamic in pursuit of our sustainability goals and create a positive impact through agile solutions and best practice..

Our Vision

Sange SA is a 100% black-female owned skills development organization proudly managed by a professional team of experts in the field of learning and development. Our programmes are built to grow individuals to make an impact in institutions and their communities. The metamorphosis of a caterpillar to a butterfly is a true example of our development roadmap and curriculum, and that is the ethos of who we are and what we do.

Our History

Founded in 2017,Sange SA creates sustainable value by applying a socioeconomic transformation outlook to our partnerships and initiatives. We implement training and skills development strategies that foster societal and economic growth through Education & Training, Leadership Development, Transformation & Good Governance.
